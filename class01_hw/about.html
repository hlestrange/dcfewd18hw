About Me

I'm a UI/UX designer with a passion for creating web applications and software. On a daily basis, I work as part of a team to craft delightful and user-friendly experiences from the beginning to end. I'm an avid learner and lover of experimentation and psychology, meaning I love to fully investigate the business need, conduct usability research, learn the newest creative software, and get my hands dirty with a little front-end coding. I'm most satisfied when a product simultaneously looks great, solves the client's need, and makes the user happy.
